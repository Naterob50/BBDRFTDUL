# 🏀 Draft Duel

**Draft Duel** is an interactive basketball trivia game that puts your NBA draft knowledge to the ultimate test. The premise is simple but addicting: you are presented with two NBA players, and you have to guess **who was drafted higher**. 

Think you know your lottery history? Prove it and build the ultimate streak.

---

## 🚀 Features

*   **Head-to-Head Matchups:** Dynamic pairings comparing players across different eras, draft classes, and positions.
*   **Streak Tracker:** Build your consecutive correct guess streak and see how deep your draft knowledge really goes.
*   **Detailed Player Cards:** Shows draft year, team, and actual pick number immediately after you lock in your guess.
*   **Responsive & Fast:** Optimized for quick fire rounds on mobile, tablet, or desktop.

---

## 🎮 How to Play

1. Two NBA players appear on screen.
2. Compare their draft pedigree. *Example: Paolo Banchero vs. Anthony Edwards.*
3. Click or tap on the player you believe was selected **higher (closer to Pick #1)** in their respective draft.
4. Get it right to keep your win streak alive. Get it wrong, and your streak resets to zero!
